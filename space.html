space : solar system

The solar system is a vast and dynamic collection of celestial bodies, including the Sun, eight planets, their moons, dwarf planets, asteroids, and comets.
At its center lies the Sun, a massive star that provides the necessary heat and light for life on Earth.
The planets, such as Earth, Mars, and Jupiter, orbit the Sun in elliptical paths, each with unique characteristics and atmospheres. Beyond the planets, the Kuiper Belt and Oort Cloud contain numerous icy bodies, contributing to our understanding of the origins and evolution of our solar system.
